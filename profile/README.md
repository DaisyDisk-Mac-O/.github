## Overview

DaisyDisk Advanced is a visual disk space analysis application that maps your entire storage as an interactive sunburst chart, making it immediately clear where space is being consumed and enabling fast, informed cleanup decisions.

The sunburst visualization represents every folder and file as a proportionally sized segment, giving you an intuitive spatial sense of which directories are consuming the most space without reading a list of numbers or navigating folder hierarchies blindly.

Interactive navigation lets you click into any segment to explore its contents, drilling down through nested folders until you identify the specific files responsible for any space-consuming directory with a natural, visual browsing experience.

The collection panel gathers files marked for deletion from any location into a temporary staging area, letting you review everything you plan to remove before committing to any deletions and providing a clear summary of how much space the cleanup will recover.

For anyone whose drive is filling up and who wants to understand exactly why before deciding what to delete, DaisyDisk Advanced is the visualization tool that makes storage analysis instant, intuitive, and genuinely helpful rather than a chore of scrolling through file listings.

## Performance

DaisyDisk balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
